Biography

Takayuki Omori obtained his Ph.D. in Engineering from Ritsumeikan University in Japan. He is currently an associate professor at Ritsumeikan University. He is interested in human-centric software engineering. In these years, he has been mainly working for analyzing fine-grained code changes in software development to clarify how software developers work.
He has taught software engineering, object-oriented technology and UML, distributed systems, computer architecture. He has also taught compiler, TCP/IP network, robot programming, logic circuit, and hardware (single-board computer) in classroom experiments.
